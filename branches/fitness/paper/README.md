**Paper entry for the fitness branch inside the AKM mother hub.**

## Paper Position

This branch is written as a method and systems paper for fitness planning under real constraints.
It is not presented as a broad outcome-validation paper and does not claim general effectiveness across users.

## Evidence Scope

The evidence model is `n=1 longitudinal self-use`.
The relevant evidence comes from long-term use under stable constraints, structured state records, training logs, and external behavior checks.

## Reading Note

Read this paper as a constrained decision-system case.
The core claim is methodological: AKM can turn training advice into profile-first, state-dependent planning.
