## Scope

The DaE paper documents how AKM is implemented in persona-aware advisory and collaboration workflows.
It focuses on method structure, upstream asset design, and the role of `PersonaProfile` as a reusable collaboration asset.

## What This Paper Covers

- AKM applied to persona-aware collaboration
- the methodological role of `PersonaProfile`
- why DaE is a reusable system component rather than a one-off prompt pattern
