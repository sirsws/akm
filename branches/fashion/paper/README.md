**Paper entry for the fashion branch inside the AKM mother hub.**

## Paper Position

This branch is written as a method paper about wardrobe planning and outfit decisions under real scene and asset constraints.
It is not presented as image-recognition validation, large-sample benchmarking, or a general aesthetics paper.

## Reading Note

Read this paper as a decision-system paper:
its claim is that styling improves when upstream modeling happens before outfit generation.
